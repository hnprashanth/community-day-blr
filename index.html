After successful event in 2017, AWS Bengaluru community is back with another mega event. Community has already made great progress in roping in bunch of great speakers, final list will be announced shortly. The event is expected to have about five hundred attendees.

Event Information

This is a Bengaluru based group for anyone interested in Amazon Web Services, Cloud Computing, AWS design implementation and servicing, EC2, S3, High Scalability Computing, Real Life uses of AWS and cloud servers, SaaS, IAAS, PAAS and many more.

All skills levels are welcome. We started this group because to meet other outdoor enthusiasts. Looking forward to exploring the outdoors with everybody. Let's get together for affordable activities in our neighborhood.

Reasons To Attend

  • Hear from the successes of your peers and enhance your skills and gain knowledge for developing AWS practice
  • Socialize with your peers over a cup of coffee or Lunch during event
  • Meet and learn from community experts of Bengaluru about the latest trends in AWS Cloud
  • Get familiar with upcoming trends like Serverless Architecture, Machine Learning, DevOps.
  • Meet and share your knowledge with the budding AWS community of Gujarat
  • Explore AWS tools and offerings by various organizations
  • Win AWS Goodies
